Countering violent extremism and mass casualty concerns:
An introduction for education staff

This video was developed to support school leaders understand and identify why young people are at risk of radicalisation to violent extremism and mass casualty violence.

School leaders can use this video to further understand:

  • the current threat landscape,
  • how schools are best placed to support student engagement and wellbeing,
  • possible indicators of concerns,
  • what to do when concerns are observed ; and
  • where support can be accessed.

If you have concerns about a young person at risk of violent extremism or mass casualty violence, consult with your existing pathways and follow Violent extremism reporting processes.

More information on CVE and mass casualty concerns is available here.
